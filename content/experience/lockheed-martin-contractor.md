I joined Lockheed Martin in June 2023 as a contractor, working as a Senior Software Engineer on next-generation technology development for infrared search and track systems. The role focused on building low-latency computer vision and AI capabilities on a custom FPGA-based compute platform while working close to the hardware and system architecture.

During this phase, I drove implementation work in C/C++ for low-latency CV and AI pipelines, developed advanced mathematics and geospatial algorithms to improve precision, and contributed to the technical foundation that supported real-time sensing and analysis workflows.
